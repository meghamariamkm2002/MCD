Mixing to your linear programs or mills, having widely used in logistics, management and production planning, modern mill solvers typically employ a branch and cut framework that utilizes a branch and bound tree search procedure combined with cutting plane algorithms to solve mixing to your linear programs.
This paper presents a machine learning approach to accelerate mill solvers by intelligently configuring cutting plane algorithms, also known as separators.
Modern mill absorbers implement various separators such as Gomorrian clique to generate cutting planes to tighten LP solutions within each branch and bound node.
At different separators are designed to exploit different structures for different mill instances, but learning to activate the activated separators for different instances were able to significantly accelerate mill solvers.
