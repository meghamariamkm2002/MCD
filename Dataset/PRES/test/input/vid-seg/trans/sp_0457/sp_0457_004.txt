The intuition of our framework is that we only apply the zero-order optimization on the most needed parts of VFL, that's the output layer of the client, and all other parts of VFL using the first-order optimization.
With this framework, we not only leverage the privacy protection of zero-order optimization,
but also we applied the zero of optimization on the minimal parts of VFL, which significantly relieved the slow convergence problem from the pure zero of order optimization with VFL.
