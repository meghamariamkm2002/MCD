Here is the pipeline of our work.
As shown in the figure A, we first divide the input space into polyhedrons with oblique decision tree.
A polyhedron is a subspace whose boundaries are hyperplanes for each sample.
As shown in the figure B, we build an attention model to calculate each polyhedron's attention on the sample with a novel
attention mechanism, then attention scores are regarded as weights to calculate the trainable value functions weighted summation, which is output of the polyhedron attention module.
For the fitted polyhedron attention module, we propose an interpretable
framework to extract the contribution of different interactions on model prediction.
In the following presentation, I will first introduce our attention mechanism and then I will briefly introduce our interpretation framework.
