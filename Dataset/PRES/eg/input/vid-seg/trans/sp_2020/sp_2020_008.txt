In this work, our contribution can be concluded in the following three parts, which will firstly explore the adversarial vulnerability across pre-trained and finite VL models.
To solve this problem, we propose a VL attack method to attack from different levels, and we conduct extensive experiments on five VL models and six tasks.
For the limitation, currently our recent problem is formulated by assuming the pre-trained model and the downstream model share the similar structures.
The adversarial transferability between the different pre-trained and finite models is worth exploring, which we left it to the future study.
