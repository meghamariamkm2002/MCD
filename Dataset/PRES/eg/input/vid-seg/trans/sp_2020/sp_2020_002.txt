The recent success of vain language pre-trained models on multimodal tasks has denied widespread attention in both academics and industry.
However, a potential security risk is also exposed.
where malicious third party may use the public pre-trained vain language model to craft adversarial samples and use them to attack the different downstream task models, even they don't know the parameters and structure of this model.
So in this work, we ask the following question.
Can we generate the adversarial perturbations on a pre-trained VM model to attack the various black box downstream task model, fine-tuned on the pre-trained one?
